Our Purpose

Our Vision & Mission

We stand on the foundation of a dream to create an inclusive learning ecosystem across Indonesia.

Vision

To create an Indonesia where every child has access to quality education and the opportunity to reach their full potential by 2045.

Mission

Empowering communities through education by connecting youth mentors with children in need, fostering growth and creating lasting impact.

Our Culture

History

Milestones & Growth

Key moments that shaped our movement

2018

The Beginning

Founded with a hopeful idea to empower communities through education, starting our journey in South Tangerang, Indonesia.

2019

Expanding Reach

Grew our volunteers to 50+ members and taught English, Maths, Science to children in a remote area in South Tangerang, Indonesia.

2020

Going Digital

Adapted to virtual learning, reaching students across Indonesia despite pandemic challenges and started our first scholarship webinar.

2021

Global Network

Welcomed international contributors for our webinars from 10 countries with more diverse topics, building a truly global community.

2025

Moving Forward

Reached 1,000+ volunteers and 1,000+ activities. Continue to promote SDGs, and shift to be a global self-development platform to help young people to reach their potentials.
